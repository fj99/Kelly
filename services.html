Services

I am a certified Grief Coach with the State of Connecticut. I am in my first year of practice. I completed the course “From Grief to Gratitude” with Dora Carpenter of DC, on November 19, 2021. I have obtained the tools to be able to assist you not only from the course, but through life’s experiences. I have also obtained the wisdom that goes hand in hand with this process. Please let me guide you through your grief process.

I offer a five-week course on grief

There are numerous work sheets that help with the grieving process. Sessions are an hour either by phone/virtual/or in person Sessions are an hour long once a week. The fee is $500.00 and is not covered by insurance. If you need further care I have resources that will assist you.
